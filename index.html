Expert Evaluation

A one-off app review is a quick, risk-free way to get the benefits of my experience without committing to an extended engagement.

I stand behind my work: if you're not happy with the results, I'll gladly refund the evaluation fee.

Advising

Ongoing advising on product design and development is the easiest way to leverage my expertise without hiring me to create the whole thing.

By identifying design and development problems early on, I'll help your team work more effectively and deliver a more user-friendly product in less time. I'll also help refine your process to cut down on miscommunication and bottlenecks, and introduce useful best practices that will make the whole process run more smoothly.

Details: Monthly retainer. I'll spend two hours a week meeting with your team, during which I'll help identify technical and UX problems, work with you to establish and refine a user-centered development process, and make sure you're on track to put your best foot forward at launch.
